TETRIS MEETS
BITCOIN MINING.

Clear rows, then tap Space to “mine” a block. No crypto wallet needed. No setup. Just press Start and play through 15 short levels.

How to play

  1. Stack blocks with the arrow keys (or touch buttons).
  2. Clear a row to unlock the mining step.
  3. Press Space to search for a winning hash.
  4. Beat all 15 levels to earn 5 bonus sats saved on your device.

Bonus sats are just for fun on this phone or computer — not real money yet.
